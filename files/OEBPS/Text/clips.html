 Clips

— Saving Text —

Clips

Clips allow you to save snippets of text and paste them into your document at any time.

It can also be used to insert tags around your text.


Clipboard History

The Clipboard History allows quick access to your most recently copied or cut text using Edit→Paste From Clipboard History.
