Tutorials

— How-To Guides —

These tutorials walk you through step by step how to create your first EPUB and how to do common tasks in Sigil.

The tutorials are not updated for Sigil 0.7.x.

Details and tips on all of Sigil's features can be found in the Features chapter.

To give you an idea of what is involved in making an EPUB, here is an overview of the steps you might follow:

  • What is EPUB?
  • Convert your document to HTML (if it's not an EPUB already) so it can be loaded into Sigil
  • Enter book details including Author and Title
  • Add a cover image
  • Identify your chapters
  • Split your chapters into separate files in the EPUB for easier reading
  • Create a Table of Contents
  • Set your first text file (where the book will open) as semantic type Text
  • Check for spelling mistakes
  • Create footnotes if needed
  • Validate your ebook against the EPUB standards
  • Test your TOC, footnotes, links
  • Check your stylesheets are linked, remove unused images or styles
  • Test on actual ereaders – what works in one may not work on another

As a first step – why not download the EPUB version of the Sigil User Guide and load it into Sigil. You can then look around and see what a typical EPUB file looks like

You can also do this with any EPUB you have, unless it is locked by DRM.
